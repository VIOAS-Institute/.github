## Vèlyntaina Institute of Ontology and Anomalous Studies (VIOAS Institute)
_„Researching Exceptional Phenomena and Discrepancies of Reality and Nature“_

- The VIOAS Institute (aka. VIOAS) is an atemporal and **fictional** research institute that deals with anomalies affecting reality, nature, and us people in general. We are involved in investigating the Backrooms, but we also specialize in other mysterious ontological and natural anomalies.

- We are not bound to a specific game and have no fixed timeline, we will however have a regulated canonical law. In some ways, we are comparable to the ASYNC Research Institute and the SCP Foundation. We operate through shared principles and structures, while developing and maintaining our own canonical framework.
